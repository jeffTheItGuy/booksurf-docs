# Platform Overview

> **What is Booksurf?**
> Booksurf is a modern bookmark management platform that allows users to organize, share, and discover web resources through an intuitive folder-based system.

## Key Capabilities

- **Organized Storage:** Create folders to categorize bookmarks by topic, project, or purpose
- **Smart Search:** Semantic search powered by AI to find relevant content quickly
- **Community Discovery:** Explore public folders shared by other users
- **Track Stats:** View stats and click stats for a folder
- **Follow Folders:** Follow your favourite folders
- **Download Folders:** Load downloaded folders into the Booksurf extension
- **Voting System:** Upvote quality content to help others discover the best resources

## Use Cases

**For Researchers** — Organize research papers, articles, and resources by topic or project.

**For Developers** — Save code snippets, documentation, and tutorials in organized collections.

**For Content Creators** — Curate inspiration and reference materials for your creative work.

**For Teams** — Share resource collections with team members and collaborate.

**For Affiliates** — Share your affiliate links and market your products.

**For Job Postings** — Share job posting links and aggregate potential talented employees.
