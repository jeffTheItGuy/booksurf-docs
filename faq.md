# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Booksurf.

---

## Account & Membership

**Is Booksurf free to use?**
Yes! Booksurf is completely free to use. You can create folders and bookmarks, use semantic search, and access all core features without any subscription or payment.

**How can I become a member?**
By donating any amount above $3, you get a membership card. Membership benefits include:
- Priority processing of folders during moderation
- Your public folder requests are automatically reset to 5 when you complete payment
- Your maximum private folders is upgraded to 5
- Your folders get a ranking boost in the featured tab

Note: Paying again before membership expires resets the membership, it doesn't extend it.

**Do I need to create an account to use Booksurf?**
Yes, you need a free account to save folders and bookmarks. However, you can browse public folders without an account. Creating an account takes less than a minute.

**Can I change my username or email?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Edit Profile → update your username or email → Save. You may need to verify a new email address before the change takes effect.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Account Settings → scroll to "Delete Account" in the Danger Zone section → follow the confirmation steps.

When you delete your account, your private bookmarks are removed immediately and your public folders are hidden from public view. Your account enters a 30-day grace period before permanent deletion — during which you can recover it if you change your mind.

> ⚠️ After 30 days, the account and all remaining data are permanently and irreversibly deleted.

**Can I recover my account after deleting it?**
Yes — you have a **30-day window** to recover your account. Go to the login page, click "Recover Account", and enter your credentials to confirm your identity. Your account will be restored and you'll be logged in automatically.

Your former public folders will be restored as private. Unfortunately, former private folders cannot be recovered as they are permanently deleted immediately when you delete your account.

> After 30 days, recovery is no longer possible and you will need to create a new account.

## Features & Usage

**How many folders and bookmarks can I create?**

For basic users:
- 3 private folders
- Maximum of 15 bookmarks per folder

For members:
- 5 private folders
- Maximum of 20 bookmarks per folder

**What's the difference between private and public folders?**

Private Folders:
- Only visible to you
- Not discoverable by other users
- Cannot be shared (unless you change to public)
- Used for making personal collections before requesting to make public

Public Folders:
- Visible to all Booksurf users
- Discoverable via search and browse
- Can be upvoted, followed, and downloaded
- Used for sharing resources with the community

**How can I share my folders?**
You cannot directly share your private folders. You have to first request to make them public. Once public, click the share icon to get a shareable link.

**What are Public Requests?**
Public Request count is the number of times you can request to change a private folder to a public folder. The count resets every month, or immediately if you become a member.

**How can I edit my private folders?**
You can edit private folders in two ways:

*Booksurf Manager (recommended for power users):*
- Navigate to the Booksurf Manager in the header section
- Drag and drop, reorder, copy, move, and delete your bookmarks
- Remember to Save after you are done to persist the changes

*Edit Folder Modal:*
- In My Folders, click the settings icon on the folder
- Update the folder in the form and save after changes are complete

**Can I edit my public folders?**
No. A snapshot is created of a private folder when it is upgraded to public view. It cannot be edited.

## Troubleshooting

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Follow the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

**I accidentally deleted a folder. Can I recover it?**
Unfortunately, deleted folders cannot be recovered. Once you delete a folder, it's permanently removed from our servers. We recommend downloading important folders as backups before deleting them.

**Why can't I find my private folders when searching?**
Private folders are only visible to you and only appear in your personal dashboard. They are intentionally excluded from public search results to maintain your privacy. Go to My Folders → Private Folders to find them.

## Privacy & Security

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We follow industry best practices for data security. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for full details.

**Do you sell my data?**
No. We do not and will never sell your personal information to third parties. We don't use tracking cookies or share your data with advertisers.

**Can other users see my private folders?**
No. Private folders are completely hidden from other users. They cannot be discovered through search, browsing, or any other means.

**What data do you collect?**
We collect only what's necessary to provide the service: your email, username, and the folders/bookmarks you create. We use essential cookies only to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies or collect browsing data outside of Booksurf. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for complete details.

## Content Policy

**What types of links are not allowed?**
The following are strictly prohibited:
- Adult/pornographic content
- Copyrighted material without authorization
- Illegal content
- Malware or phishing sites
- Child exploitation material (zero tolerance)
- Graphic violence or extreme gore
- Hate speech
- Illegal drugs or substances

Violating these rules results in immediate removal and possible account termination. See our [Content Policy](content-policy.md) for full details.

**How do I report inappropriate content?**
Click the "Report" button on the folder or bookmark. You can also email us at mail@nimbusurf.com with the subject line "Content Policy Violation" and include the link to the content. We review all reports promptly.

**What happens if I violate the content policy?**
First violations typically result in content removal and a warning. Repeated or severe violations (like illegal content) result in immediate account termination.
