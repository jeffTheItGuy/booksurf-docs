# Getting Started

> **Welcome to Booksurf!**
> Follow these simple steps to get started with organizing and discovering web resources.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up for a free Booksurf account to start organizing your bookmarks.

1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Choose a unique username
4. Verify your email address (check your inbox)

## Step 2: Create Your First Folder

Organize your bookmarks by creating folders for different topics or projects.

1. In the header, click the Create Folder button "➕"
2. Give your folder a descriptive title
3. Add a detailed description to provide context
4. Add relevant tags to help with organization
5. Add the minimum required bookmarks to it

## Step 3: Add Your Bookmarks

Start adding links to your folders to build your collection.

1. In My Folders, navigate to a private folder you've created and click the Edit Button "⚙️"
2. Click "Add Bookmark" or the "+" button
3. Paste the URL you want to save
4. Add a title
5. Repeat to add more bookmarks
6. Save the bookmark to your folder

## Step 4: Share Your Folder

Make your folder public and share it with the community.

1. In My Folders, navigate to the private folders section
2. Click on the Request to Make Public button "🌐"
3. Confirm the action and wait for moderation
4. Once your folder is public you can see it in the public folders section in My Folders

## Step 5: Discover & Explore

Use powerful search and discovery features to find relevant content.

1. Use semantic search to find folders by meaning, not just keywords
2. Browse public folders by tags or categories
3. Check the "For You" section for personalized recommendations
4. Upvote quality folders to help others discover them
5. Follow folders you find useful

> 🎉 **You're Ready!** Explore the [How-To Guides](how-to.md) for detailed instructions on specific features, or check out the [FAQ](faq.md) if you have questions.
