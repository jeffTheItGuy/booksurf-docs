# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2024

> Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Booksurf.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Information:** Username, email address, password (hashed)
- **Content:** Folders and bookmarks you create
- **Essential Cookies:** Only to maintain your login session

## 2. How We Use Your Information

**Service Provision** — To provide and maintain our bookmark management service.

**Account Management** — To create and manage your account.

**Security** — To detect and prevent fraud and security issues.

## 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

- **Public Content:** Folders you mark as public are visible to all users
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law

## 4. Data Security

- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- **Password Security:** Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Access Controls:** Restricted access to personal information

## 5. Your Rights

**Access** — View your personal information through your account settings.

**Correction** — Update your information through your account settings.

**Deletion** — Delete your account and all associated data at any time.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information only while your account is active. When you request account deletion:

- **Immediately:** Your private bookmarks are permanently deleted and your public folders are hidden from public view.
- **30-day grace period:** Your account enters a pending deletion state. You can recover your account at any time during this window by logging in and confirming your identity. Your public folders will be restored upon recovery.
- **After 30 days:** Your account and all remaining data are permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems. Recovery is no longer possible after this point.

Note: Private bookmarks cannot be recovered even within the grace period, as they are permanently deleted at the moment of your deletion request.

## 7. Children's Privacy

> ⚠️ Booksurf is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Third-Party Links

Booksurf contains user-submitted links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

## 9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "Last Updated" date indicates when the policy was last revised.
