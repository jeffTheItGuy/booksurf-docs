# Content Policy

> **Our Commitment**
> We are committed to maintaining a safe and legal platform. This service does not host any content; users may only submit external links. We promptly remove any link that violates our content policy.

---

## Prohibited Content

Links to the following are strictly prohibited:

- **Copyrighted material** — content shared without authorization from rights holders
- **Illegal content** — material that violates applicable laws
- **Adult/pornographic content** — any sexually explicit material
- **Malware or phishing sites** — sites designed to harm users or steal information
- **Child exploitation material** — any content depicting minors in sexual contexts (zero tolerance)
- **Violence or gore** — graphic violent content or extreme gore
- **Hate speech** — content promoting violence or hatred against groups
- **Drugs or illegal substances** — sites promoting illegal drug sales or use

---

## Our Policy

**User-Submitted Links Only** — We do not host or store any files. Users are solely responsible for the links they share.

**Zero Tolerance for Illegal Content** — Links to illegal content are immediately removed and accounts are permanently banned.

**Proactive Monitoring** — We actively monitor and remove prohibited content, even without reports.

**Immediate Removal** — Reported violations are reviewed and removed promptly.

**Repeat Offender Policy** — Accounts repeatedly violating our policy will be permanently terminated.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you find content that violates our policy, please report it by sending a notice containing:

1. Your contact information (name and email address)
2. The specific link(s) on our site that violate our policy
3. The type of violation (copyright, adult content, illegal material, etc.)
4. For copyright claims: identification of the copyrighted work and proof of ownership
5. A statement that the information in your report is accurate
6. Your signature (physical or electronic)

**Email:** mail@nimbusurf.com
Please include "Content Policy Violation" in the subject line and specify the type of violation.

---

## How We Handle Reports

**Immediate Review** — All reports are reviewed promptly, with priority given to illegal content.

**Content Removal** — Valid reports result in immediate removal of the violating link.

**Account Action** — The user who submitted the link is notified and may face account suspension or termination.

**Reporter Notification** — We notify the reporter that action has been taken.

---

> ⚠️ **Important:** Submitting false reports may result in account termination. We cooperate with law enforcement regarding illegal content. Child exploitation material is reported to relevant authorities.
